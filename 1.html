1. How to I know this marvelous team

When I was in middle school and high school,
The Korean player named Park Ji-sung played for a team called Manchester United in EPL.
I happened to encounter Chelsea while watching Park Ji-sung's game.


Chelsea then came as a big shock to me.
Fantastic, aggressive, thick-boned, unsophisticated but powerful.
The highest ranking at the time team as Arsenal and Manchester United were like peacocks
but Chelsea were like bears.
